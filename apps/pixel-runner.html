Cat Escape browser game by Sanjin Pepic

Cat Escape is a lightweight pixel runner game created as a fast product experiment. The project demonstrates rapid prototyping, interaction design, engagement loops, and minimal-scope MVP delivery in the browser.

CAT ESCAPE.EXE Distance: 0m

Jump with Space, W, Up, or tap/click. Avoid office cacti forever.

Built by Sanjin Pepic as a rapid experiment in product design and retention mechanics.
